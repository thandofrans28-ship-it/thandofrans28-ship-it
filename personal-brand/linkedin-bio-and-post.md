## 🚀 First post — "Introduce yourself + Pathfinda"

> **I'm 17, I'm in matric, and I just shipped an app to the Play Store. Here's why. 🧭**
>
> Around me, I kept watching classmates make one of the biggest decisions of their lives —
> what to study — with almost no real information. A rushed conversation, a pile of
> prospectuses, and a lot of guessing. Get it wrong and you lose a year and a lot of money.
>
> So I built **Pathfinda**.
>
> It's a mobile app that helps South African students find their path: you enter your
> subjects, your marks and your interests, and it maps you to degrees you actually qualify
> for, the careers they lead to, and where to study them. I designed it, built it in
> Flutter, and took it all the way through to closed testing on Google Play — solo.
>
> I'm heading into **Actuarial Science** in 2027, and honestly Pathfinda is that same
> instinct applied early: take messy, high-stakes decisions and make them clearer with data.
>
> Next up: bursary matching, so students find the funding they qualify for too.
>
> If you work in actuarial science, data, or ed-tech — or you just remember how confusing
> Grade 12 was — I'd love to connect. 👇
