## ✅ How to work the tracker

1. **Open the Notion database** and sort by *Deadline (est.)* ascending.
2. For each **High** priority row, open the link and **confirm the real deadline** today.
3. Set **Status → In progress** the moment you start an application.
4. Prep a reusable pack once: certified ID, latest results, proof of income (for NSFAS),
   a short motivation letter (use your 200-word story), and your CV.
5. Apply to **every** one you qualify for — bursaries are a numbers game.
